Customer Service

Overview

Knowing the proper way to interact with others is a very important skill one must have. You should always display punctuality, dedication, professionalism, hospitability, and courtesy while interacting with others. It gives a positive perception of your personal brand. While interacting with customers, good customer service skills gives the same positive perception. It will demonstrate that your company’s brand is consumer oriented, which leads to the satisfaction and happiness of your consumer market. If your customers are happy they will continue to utilize your service and make you and your company happy.



When interacting with customers, one must keep in mind three things: proper and professional greeting, active listening, and proper and professional responding.

• Proper and professional greetings are a “welcome” or a “hello” upon a guest approaching you or entering your work space. This is also paired with a smile or a positive facial expression which welcomes the guest through nonverbal communication.
• Active listening consists of what we call here at Indeed Tech, SLANT. Sit Up, Listen, Ask Questions, Nod, and Take Notes. When SLANT is performed correctly, it gives off the impression that the guest is the center of your attention when they are speaking.
• Proper and professional responses entails of accurately analyzing the situation or problem and approaching it with the best possible idea or ideas you may have. Letting the guest know what you are going to do for them to assist them and let them know you are a valuable resource for them.
